About the Song

Origins

Pan-eul Dwijib-eo (판을 뒤집어), translated as The Tables Have Turned, is an original Korean song by Shaunak Okhde released in 2026. Written entirely in Korean, the song embraces the energy and confidence of modern K-Pop while focusing on determination and transformation.

Themes

Throughout the song, the lyrics speak with confidence and self-belief. Victory is treated not as a distant goal, but as something already within reach. The song encourages listeners to trust themselves and create their own future.

Meaning

The phrase "판을 뒤집어" literally means "to turn the table over" or "flip the game". Throughout the song, this symbolizes changing circumstances and taking control of one's destiny.

Global Experience
